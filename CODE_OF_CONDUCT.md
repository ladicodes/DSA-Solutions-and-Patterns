# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give helpful, actionable feedback
- Assume good intent
- Keep discussions focused on learning and improvement

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- Discrimination or hateful language
- Trolling, intimidation, or hostile behavior
- Sharing private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and any project-related community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

If behavior violates this Code of Conduct, maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from participation.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private issue if possible or contact the maintainer directly through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as fairly and promptly as possible.
